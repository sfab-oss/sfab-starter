The signed-out entry point: the shared `AuthShell` frame over an email + password
card, validated with zod. One of the three auth surfaces (with **Sign up** and
**Accept invite**) that share the same centered shell.

<BlockViewer name="sign-in" />

## What it demonstrates

- **`AuthShell`** — the centered, sidebar-less frame shared by every auth route.
- **`SignInForm`** — RHF + `zodResolver` with inline field errors.
- **Cross-link** — a "Sign up" link, inert in the gallery; wire it to your router.
