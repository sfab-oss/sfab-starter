Replaces the fake revenue dashboard. **Today** is the verb-first operator landing:
hero actions (muted until Transaction Core), up to three KPI tiles with honest
empty states, deep-link-style action items, and a capped inventory movements feed
— distinct from document activity.

<BlockViewer name="today-overview" />

## What it demonstrates

- **`PrimaryActionBar`** — New sale / Collect payment (disabled with explanation pre-TC).
- **`TodayKpiTile`** — live low-stock count + honest-empty AR and sales tiles.
- **`TodayActionItems`** — invoices to collect and low-stock prompts.
- **`InventoryMovementsFeed`** — labeled interim manual-movement table.
