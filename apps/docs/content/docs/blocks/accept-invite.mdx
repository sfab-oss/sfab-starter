Invite-acceptance surface: the shared `AuthShell` frame over a card that shows the
organization, invited email, and role, then collects a name + password. One of the
three auth surfaces (with **Sign in** and **Sign up**) that share the same centered
shell.

<BlockViewer name="accept-invite" />

## What it demonstrates

- **`AuthShell`** — the centered, sidebar-less frame shared by every auth route.
- **`AcceptInviteForm`** — org/role context props + RHF + `zodResolver` form.
- **Token-driven props** — resolve org, email, and role from the invite server-side.
